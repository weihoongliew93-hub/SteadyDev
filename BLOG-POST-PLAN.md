## Writing Guidelines for Authentic Content

### ✅ DO:
- Use your actual experiences (POS system, printing module, etc.)
- Talk about patterns you've observed across multiple projects
- Share your methodologies and frameworks
- Use phrases like "I've seen," "In my experience," "Common patterns include"
- Anonymize client details while keeping technical reality intact
- Be specific about technical details
- Share your decision-making process

### ❌ DON'T:
- Create fictional case studies with specific numbers
- Invent client scenarios that didn't happen
- Make up ROI calculations without real data
- Claim specific results you haven't witnessed
- Use "A client saved X amount" without real data
- Fabricate timelines or metrics
