Frequently Asked Questions

How do payments work?

I require a 50% deposit upfront to secure your spot and begin work. The remaining 50% is due upon project completion. I accept bank transfers in RM or USD.

What if I'm not satisfied with the work?

You'll receive regular updates throughout the project. Before final payment, you have a review period to ensure everything meets your expectations. If you cancel mid-project, you'll receive a prorated refund based on work completed.

What's your typical response time?

For initial inquiries, I typically respond within 1-2 business days. For urgent/express enquiries, I respond within 24 hours. During active projects, I provide regular updates and respond to questions within 24-48 hours.

Do you offer refunds or guarantees?

Yes. If no work has started, you'll receive a full refund. For mid-project cancellations, refunds are prorated based on work completed. I don't offer refunds after final delivery, but you have a review period before the final payment.

How do you handle intellectual property?

All code and work product becomes your property upon final payment. Your existing code and business information remain strictly confidential. I can sign NDAs if required.

What happens after the free consultation?

You'll receive a detailed written assessment via email, including my recommendations, estimated timeline, and pricing. There's no obligation to proceed — the consultation and assessment are completely free.

Do you provide 24/7 emergency support?

No, I don't provide 24/7 emergency support. My availability is limited to evenings and weekends (Malaysia Time, GMT+8). For urgent issues requiring immediate response, please seek dedicated on-call support services.
