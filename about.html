About SteadyDevs

Helping businesses maintain and improve legacy systems with careful, reliable approach

The Problem

Many businesses rely on systems built years ago that are difficult to maintain. Documentation is missing, original developers have moved on, and every change feels risky. When something breaks or needs improvement, finding someone willing to carefully work with these systems is challenging.

That's Where SteadyDevs Comes In

SteadyDevs specializes in working with legacy .NET systems and complex codebases that others find intimidating. The focus is on understanding how your system works, fixing issues carefully, and making improvements without breaking what's already functioning.

Our Experience

For over 11 years, the team has been deeply immersed in building and maintaining cloud-based point-of-sale systems built on .NET. This extended tenure with complex platforms has provided comprehensive expertise in retail business logic, from transaction processing and inventory management to payment workflows and multi-location operations. The team understands the intricacies of mission-critical systems where downtime isn't an option and every change must be carefully validated.

What We Bring

  • Deep expertise in cloud-based POS systems — 11+ years dedicated to .NET-based point-of-sale platforms, developing comprehensive knowledge of retail business logic, transaction processing, inventory management, and payment workflows in production environments
  • Strong debugging and analysis skills — Methodical approach to understanding complex systems and tracing issues to their root cause
  • Careful approach to system changes — Every change is made with consideration of existing functionality and potential impacts
  • Clear communication — Regular updates and detailed explanations of what was done and why

Setting Expectations

SteadyDevs operates as a part-time service focused on structured fixes and non-critical support rather than real-time emergency response.

What this means:

  • Part-time availability (see contact page for details)
  • Projects are planned and scoped before work begins
  • Response within 24-48 hours for inquiries
  • Best suited for planned improvements, bug fixes, and system analysis rather than urgent production emergencies
