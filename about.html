The Problem & Where We Come In

The Problem

Many businesses rely on systems built years ago that are difficult to maintain. Documentation is missing, original developers have moved on, and every change feels risky. When something breaks or needs improvement, finding someone willing to carefully work with these systems is challenging.

That's Where SteadyDevs Comes In

SteadyDevs specializes in working with legacy .NET systems and complex codebases that others find intimidating. The focus is on understanding how your system works, fixing issues carefully, and making improvements without breaking what's already functioning.

Our Experience

For over 11 years, the team has been deeply immersed in building and maintaining cloud-based point-of-sale systems built on .NET. This extended tenure with complex platforms has provided comprehensive expertise in retail business logic, from transaction processing and inventory management to payment workflows and multi-location operations. The team understands the intricacies of mission-critical systems where downtime isn't an option and every change must be carefully validated.
